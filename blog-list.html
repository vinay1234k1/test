WHO I AM?

Let me introduce myself, I'm Laura H. Bazán from Mexico. I am an art historian and independent art curator. I know... These days, everyone is a curator—or should I say data analyst? When I was a child, art filled my days with colors, shapes, and beauty. So, I decided to dedicate my life to it. Art, for me, is the greatest human creation. Like Charles Saatchi, I declare myself an Artholic too. But right now, I'm uncertain if I want to continue professionally in this field. This year, I moved to Italy for a fresh start, and I'm currently exploring tech skills while living abroad. I started this blog to learn to code and share my journey in this ancient city. I will write about Italian culture, museums, local tips, and more. I hope you enjoy reading my posts and exploring Rome with me.
